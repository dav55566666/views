Mackintosh

Apple has grown from a garage into a 2 billion company with 4,000 employees. After then when Steve was 30, he with his friend created the first Macintosh.

Company growth

New Apple computers scattered around the world with a circulation of 5 million copies. As a result, already at the age of 25, Steve Jobs became a millionaire.

Family

While attending a lecture at the Stanford Business School in 1989, Stephen accidentally met bank employee Lauren Powell. A year later, the young people got married, and in 1991 their son Reed was born. Lauren later gave birth to two more daughters - Erin (1995) and Eve (1998).

Iphone

The first iPhone hit stores in the summer of 2007.

Dead
