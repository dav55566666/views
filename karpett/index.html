Maintenance and Service

Professional floor mats are mostly made with yarn that is HIGH-Twist and heat set. That means that dust and dirt are sucked down and trapped. By only vacuuming, you can remove only 10% of the dirt inside the mat. When the mat is full it won't be efficient anymore. For a small fee, we clean the mats professionally on a weekly basis.
